  • Sarangkot Paragliding

    Sarangkot is a popular paragliding destination located in Pokhara, Nepal. It is a scenic hilltop that provides a panoramic view of the Annapurna mountain range and Pokhara valley.   • Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave

Visitors have to navigate through a narrow passage and climb down a steep staircase to reach the main shrine. The cave is adorned with various statues and sculptures of Hindu gods and goddesses.   • PumdiKot
